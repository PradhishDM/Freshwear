Mattifying cleanser with sugarcane and mandelic acid

DESCRIPTION

Sugarcane Soak is a gentle foaming gel cleanser enriched with antioxidant-rich sugarcane and stimulating mandelic acid. Its mild formula can be used everyday and helps remove oil from your pores.

HOW TO USE

Wet your face and neck. Take a coin-sized amount into the palm of your hand. Massage gently onto your face and neck using a circular motion. Rinse thoroughly. Pat dry.

WHEN TO USE

Anytime - AM and/or PM

HOW TO STORE

Store in a cool and dry place. Avoid exposure to direct sunlight.

Key Ingredients

Sugarcane Extract

One of India’s most abundant crops, Sugarcane (shuug.uh.kayn) is popularly used for its juice and to make sugar and jaggery. In skincare, it is known to be a source of glycolic acid which helps to gently mattify the skin and boost its natural, healthy glow.

Mandelic Acid

Mandelic Acid (man.del.ik æs.id) is a naturally-derived chemical compound. Its mattifying and antibacterial properties are powerful yet gentle on the skin: known to remove the outer layer of dead skin and help to reduce the appearance of dark spots, leaving you with smooth, soft skin.
